Find Your Perfect Match

This section introduces the core concept of the campaign. It forges a deep, relatable connection between the satisfaction of discovering the ideal meal and the universal search for a perfect partner.

Ad Concept: "She Doesn't Understand Me Like You Do"

The campaign's anchor is a humorous and relatable TV spot that personifies the QSR's core product as the ultimate, understanding companion.

Campaign Strategy Pillars

This section details the campaign's strategic foundation, built on four key pillars designed to work in synergy.

The 'Perfect Match' Swipe Game (In-App)

A central, interactive swipe game within the QSR's mobile app allows users to 'swipe left' or 'swipe right' on meal components. The goal is to shift focus to finding the **'perfect deal'**.

  • Engaging swipe interface for intuitive preference selection.
  • Learns player preferences to suggest custom meal combinations.
  • Data feeds into CRM (The Platform) to drive cross-selling campaigns.

Social Media & User-Generated Content

Leveraging the QSR's social presence with platform-specific content that encourages participation and sharing.

  • **TikTok:** Branded audio and food challenges (e.g., #MyPerfectMatchDeal).
  • **Instagram:** Visual UGC contests and interactive Reels.
  • **UGC Amplification:** Promote hashtags and run giveaways.

Seasonal Integration & Partnerships

Tapping into major cultural moments to maximise relevance and reach.

  • **Major Summer TV Event:** TV idents and real-time social commentary.
  • **Valentine's Day:** "Perfect Pair" meal deals for couples and "Self-Love" packs for singles.

Promotional Offers & Loyalty Integration

Tailored promotions to incentivise trial and drive sales, focusing on **Average Order Value (AOV)**.

  • **"Perfect Match" Meal Deals:** Tiered bundles with high-margin items.
  • **Loyalty Perks:** Double points for campaign-related orders.
  • **Gamified Offers:** Upsell prompts designed to increase side purchases.
